How to Search in WvB?

The Submit Function will not work in WvB. After Entering your Query in Searchbox, just Select the Search Engine you want to Search in.

Why WvB?

- Less in Size
- Does not Store your Browsing History
- Complete Incognito Browsing
- Open Source

What is WvB?

WvB is a Webview Browser. As mentioned its a Webview Implementation working as a Browser. Its Totally Safe as its a System Webview. You will have to update your System Webview frequently from Play Store to keep up the Performance of Webview.
